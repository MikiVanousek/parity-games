Selecting

Click on a node or edge to select it. Click on the background to deselect. Hold
Shift
to select multiple elements. You can drag selected nodes to move them.

Edges

To create an edge, select a source node (or multiple nodes) and right click on the target. Right click on a selected edge to add bend point or Bezier control point.
